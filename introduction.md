# Introduction to Azalea Gate

**Azalea Gate** is the premier verification and management bot for Solana and EVM communities. We bridge the gap between Web3 assets and Discord communities, offering powerful token gating, automated role management, and security features.

## Why Azalea Gate?

-   **Multi-Chain Verification**: Seamlessly verify assets on **Solana**, **Ethereum**, **Polygon**, **Base**, **Optimism**, and **TON**.
-   **Advanced Gating**: Gate channels based on NFT holdings, Token balances, or Staking status.
-   **Security**: Protect your community with automated link moderation, anti-spam, and mention protection.
-   **Scalable**: Built to handle communities of any size, from small DAOs to massive NFT projects.
